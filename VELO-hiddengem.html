Token Fundamental Analysis¶

  1. Introduction to Velodrome Finance
  • Velodrome Finance serves as the foundational liquidity layer for the entire Optimism ecosystem. It was launched in June 2022 with the primary goal of incentivizing liquidity for DeFi protocols. Notably, Velodrome builds upon the groundwork established by Solidly, making necessary adjustments and enhancements to ensure its maximum potential is realized.
  • For DeFi protocols to operate effectively, liquidity is essential. However, acquiring liquidity and providing incentives for liquidity providers (LPs) can often be a challenging task. Thus, Velodrome Finance was developed to address this need. The protocol offers an appealing alternative to Solidly, going beyond just liquidity by incorporating a mechanism to balance protocol emissions and fees. This is achieved through a unique voting process. However, it is worth noting that the voting process is not flawless, as influential stakeholders and protocols can utilize their voting power to prevent fee collection from pools they have invested in. Velodrome has improved on this major drawback by ensuring unbiased voting and fair compensation for LPs in cases of impermanent losses.
  1. Velodrome Finance solutions to DeFi

  • The significance of liquidity for DeFi protocols cannot be overstated. To function effectively, DeFi protocols require a certain level of liquidity. However, there are various challenges associated with generating and incentivizing liquidity in the DeFi space. Some of these problems include:

  • Expensive Pool 2 emissions: This form of liquidity incentivization often encourages short-term investments aimed at quick profits.

  • Voter bribery: Existing LPs hold an advantage over incoming LPs, creating an issue of unfair influence.

  • Difficulty in acquiring Protocol Owned Liquidity (POL): Obtaining POL can be problematic in certain cases.

These are among the key issues facing liquidity incentivization in DeFi protocols. To address these challenges, Velodrome introduces several improvements to the Solidly ecosystem, including:

  • Linking Rewards to Emissions: Unlike Solidly, Velodrome ties rewards directly to emissions. This ensures that all voters in a particular epoch have access to rewards, regardless of when the bribe was sent. This helps establish a much-needed balance between voters and external bribe providers.
  • White-Glove Support: Velodrome Finance offers dedicated support to its partners and stakeholders after the launch. As part of this support, 3% of perpetual emissions are allocated to the Velodrome team multi-signature wallet.

Tokenomics Analysis¶

Velodrome Finance utilizes two tokens to oversee its utility and governance:

  • VELO: This is an ERC-20 utility token that serves as the protocol's primary token.
  • veVELO: This is an ERC-721 governance token represented as a non-fungible token (NFT).

The VELO token is employed to reward liquidity providers by means of emissions.

On the other hand, the veVELO token is utilized for governance purposes. Any individual holding VELO tokens has the ability to lock them in a voting-escrow mechanism and receive veVELO tokens (also referred to as veNFTs) in exchange. It is possible to add additional tokens to the veVELO NFT at any given time.

The duration of the lock period, also known as the vote-escrowed period (hence the "ve" prefix), can extend up to 4 years. This duration follows a linear relationship, as illustrated below:

Locking 100 VELO tokens for 4 years will convert them into 100 veVELO tokens. Locking 100 VELO tokens for 1 year will result in 25 veVELO tokens. As the vesting time increases, the voting power (voting weight) and rewards received by the veVELO holder also increase.

Based on the inital distribution above, there are several reaasons why Velodrome could be a hidden gem.

  • Community Allocation: The largest portion of the token distribution is allocated to the community, which indicates a strong focus on decentralization and community participation. This allocation implies that the project values the involvement and support of its community members, potentially leading to active engagement, governance rights, and potential incentives for token holders.

  • Partner protocol/DAO Allocation: A significant portion of tokens is dedicated to partner protocols and decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs). This allocation suggests that VELO aims to collaborate with other projects and foster integration within the blockchain ecosystem. Such partnerships can bring value through shared resources, increased network effects, and potentially drive adoption of VELO tokens.

  • Velodrome Team Allocation: VELO tokens allocated to the Velodrome Team demonstrate a commitment to the project's development and success. This allocation is common in token distributions to ensure the team's alignment with the project's long-term goals. However, it is essential to consider the team's experience, expertise, and dedication to the project before making investment decisions.

  • Optimism Team Allocation: The allocation for the Optimism Team signifies a collaboration or association with the Optimism protocol. Optimism is a layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum, aiming to improve scalability and reduce transaction fees. If VELO is working closely with the Optimism Team, it suggests a potential synergy between the two projects, benefiting from the advancements in layer 2 technology.

  • Genesis Liquidity Pool Allocation: The allocation for the Genesis Liquidity Pool is relatively small compared to other categories. However, it indicates the availability of liquidity for VELO tokens from the project's inception. A liquid market can provide opportunities for trading and investment activities, potentially attracting more participants to the ecosystem.
